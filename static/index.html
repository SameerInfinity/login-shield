Splunk Dashboard Queries
Copy & paste into Splunk Search

These SPL queries work with sourcetype login_shield:alert. Click a query to copy it.

📊 Alert Volume Over Time
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" | timechart count by alert_type span=1h
Click to copy
🚨 Critical Alerts (last 24h)
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" severity="critical" | table _time, username, alert_type, details, ip_address | sort -_time
Click to copy
💪 Brute Force Attacks
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" alert_type="brute_force" | stats count AS attempts, max(failed_count) AS max_fails BY username, ip_address | sort -attempts
Click to copy
🕐 Unusual Login Hours
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" alert_type="unusual_timing" | stats count BY username, login_hour | sort -count
Click to copy
🌍 Impossible Travel Events
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" alert_type="impossible_travel" | table _time, username, from_country, to_country, time_diff_minutes, ip_address | sort -_time
Click to copy
🖥 New Device / IP Detections
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" (alert_type="new_ip" OR alert_type="new_device") | stats count BY username, alert_type, ip_address | sort -count
Click to copy
👤 Top Suspicious Users
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" | stats count AS total_alerts, dc(alert_type) AS unique_alert_types, values(severity) AS severities BY username | sort -total_alerts | head 20
Click to copy
🔒 Locked Account Events
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" alert_type="account_locked" | stats count, latest(_time) AS last_attempt BY username | sort -last_attempt
Click to copy
📈 Alert Severity Distribution
sourcetype="login_shield:alert" | stats count BY severity | eval pct=round(count/totals*100,1)
